Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2021

This Privacy Policy describes how Spiritual Sword (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage information: Information such as device type, operating system version, app version, language, and basic usage data (collected through standard Apple channels).
  • Identifiers: Depending on your device and settings, we (or our service providers) may use device identifiers or similar technologies for analytics and performance.

Information from third parties

How we use information

We use information to:

  • Operate and improve the App (including performance monitoring and debugging)

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • For legal reasons: If required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • To protect rights and safety: When we believe it is necessary to protect our users, the public, or our services.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Crash logs and analytics data may be retained for a limited period depending on provider settings.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

  • Analytics controls: Your device settings may allow you to limit certain tracking or analytics (availability varies by platform).
  • Contact us: You can request access, correction, or deletion of certain information by contacting us (see “Contact” below).

Children’s privacy

The App can be used by children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information, and this app does not contain advertising, user to user communication, or any online third parties. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we’ll take appropriate steps.

International users

If you use the App outside your country, your information may be processed in locations where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above and may provide additional notice within the App or on our website if changes are significant.
